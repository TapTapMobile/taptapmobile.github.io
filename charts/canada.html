Notable data
The "apparel" niche is dominated by only three advertisers who spend a lot more on media than the rest, followed by Diet & Games.
Seeming to indicate these are areas where expertise and size of budget matter most.

Apparel is a hot market, and only served by three Advertisers that can afford to run for 365 days at a stretch. See detailed info here: They are: Zulily.cm, 6dollarshorts.com, and brilliantearth.com
